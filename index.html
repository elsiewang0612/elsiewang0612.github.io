Crashes in Kentucky 2011-2015

This map presents the statewide crashes distributions by counties for the years of 2011 through 2015. A primary objective of this map is to figure out the association between the crashes frequencies and the geographic locations. Crash rate(percentage) is calulated as total number of crashes is divided by the population for each county and various types of crashes causes.According to the ducmentations, the most crashes involves alcohol, drugs, unsafe speeding, motocycle and trucks.

  • See the map: Crashes in Kentucky 2011-2015
  • Data Source: Kentucky Transportation Cabinet (KYTC)
