2014 GHG Emissions (Natural Gas) by Local Distribution Companies

This map shows the ghg emission quantity from local distribution companies by facility and area in 2014. The data was reported to EPA by facilities as of 08/16/2015. All emission data is presented in units of metric tons of carbon dioxide equivalent using GWP's from IPCC' s AR4.

  • See the map: 2014 GHG Emissions (Natural Gas) by Local Distribution Companies
  • Data Source: EPA Facility Level Emissions

Power Plants Distribution in US

This map was built with custom JavaScript code drawing upon the Leaflet JavaScript Library. This map show all power plants fueled by coal, hydro, nuclear or any combination among them. Once the user decide the intrested place, the map will show all power plants within 500 meters. For each power plant, the map will show the the name, fuel source type and the distance between the place at where users are intrested.

  • See the map: Power Plants Distribution in US
  • Data Source: US Energy Information

Crashes in Kentucky 2011-2015

This map presents the statewide crashes distributions by counties for the years of 2011 through 2015. A primary objective of this map is to figure out the association between the crashes frequencies and the geographic locations. Crash rate(percentage) is calulated as total number of crashes is divided by the population for each county and various types of crashes causes.According to the ducmentations, the most crashes involves alcohol, drugs, unsafe speeding, motocycle and trucks.

  • See the map: Crashes in Kentucky 2011-2015
  • Data Source: Kentucky Transportation Cabinet (KYTC)
